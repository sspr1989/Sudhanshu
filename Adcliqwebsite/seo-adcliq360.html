1. Website Audit

We thoroughly analyse your site. Check page meta tags, headers, keyword density, and issues. Consider social signals and backlinks.

2. Keyword Research

Will be finding keywords relating to your business, products, or services. Google Keyword Planner, SEMrush, and Ahrefs can help you find high-volume, low-competition keywords for your content.

3. On-Page Optimization

We keyword-optimize every page. Create interesting keyword Meta descriptions. Headers (H1, H2, H3) with keywords. Making SEO-friendly URLs.

We conduct competitor analysis. A thorough competitor study can help you improve your SEO approach, seize chances, and remain ahead of the competition.
